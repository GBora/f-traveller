Imperial Civis

Description

Citizens of the Empire, and the ones that ultimately are to praise or blame for it's success.

Trained in special War Colleges which exist in only the most secure and advanced Empire cities and bases, Imperial mages receive a rich education focusing on leading troops, tactics, strategy, meele, harnesing their arcane energies into potent blasts and of course magic.

Choosing to specialise as either a Red, Black or White Mage will lead the inniatite to learning the mysteries of either Fire Magic, Necromancy or Healing Magic.

Beyond their duties in dominating the battlefield with magic, Imperial Mages also offer their counsel to the Legion's General on matters of strategy or dealing with magical phenomena, they are also asinged to War Colleges as pure scholars where they advance the Empire's understanding of magic.

Requirements

Faction Relationship: Empire Neutral

As they are the citizens of the Empire, the civis are expect to at least nominally follow it's rules and be in good standing with the Imperial Government, the local officials might not keep track of the day to the life of every countryman but high crimes and betrayals can easily lead to all rights and privileges being thrown aside as the Empire no longer considers you a citizen

Attribute: End 5+

Surviving in the empire is easier than in some other human nations but it is no easy task, one must be ready to toil either with the body or the mind.

Qualification Roll: None/Soc 5+

If born in the Empire you are automatically considered a citizen, in the cases that one wants to become a citizen or needs to due to their lands being conquered or colonised by the Empire, they can swear an Oath of Allegiance and renounce their old citizenship they will then be tested by the agents of the local governor and depending on the result be admitted or not.

Specializations
Red Mage Masters of Fire Magic, incinerating any foes of the Legions.
Black Mage Masters of Necromancy, they target the enemy leadership with curses.
White Mage Masters of Healing Magic, their healing prowes turns the Legion into an unstopable force.
