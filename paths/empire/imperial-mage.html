Imperial Mage

Description

Official battle mages of the Empire, Imperial Mages are feared in the Legions for their power and yet respected for the magical support they can offer to the troops they lead.

Trained in special War Colleges which exist in only the most secure and advanced Empire cities and bases, Imperial Mages receive a rich education focusing on leading troops, tactics, strategy, melee, harnessing their arcane energies into potent blasts and of course magic.

Choosing to specialise as either a Red, Black or White Mage will lead the inniatite to learning the mysteries of either Fire Magic, Necromancy or Healing Magic.

Beyond their duties in dominating the battlefield with magic, Imperial Mages also offer their counsel to the Legion's General on matters of strategy or dealing with magical phenomena, they are also assigned to War Colleges as pure scholars where they advance the Empire's understanding of magic.

Requirements

Faction Relationship: Empire Neutral+

Imperial Mages are part of the officer corps for the Imperial Legions, thus access to the War Colleges where the mages receive their training will only be given to loyal citizens of the Empire or exceptional foreigners who are ready to pledge themselves into the Empire's service.

Attribute: Soc 7+

Every Imperial Mage receives an officer commission after they finish their training thus, they are expected to be both a model to uphold to their troops and to be able to command them on the field of battle.

Qualification Roll: Magic 6+

Acceptance into the War Colleges and then further on into one of the Red, Black or White Councils is dependant on showing an aptitude with magic, although the training requires a hefty sum the Empire is willing to grant scholarships to those they feel are talented.

Specializations
Red Mage Masters of Fire Magic, incinerating any foes of the Legions.
Black Mage Masters of Necromancy, they target the enemy leadership with curses.
White Mage Masters of Healing Magic, their healing prowess turns the Legion into an unstoppable force.

Unfortunate Events
Roll 1d6 Description
1 You get injured in combat, roll on the Injury Table.
2 Your division gets deployed to a border skirmish with the Duchy, one of their knights feels personally slighted by your conduct, you gain a Enemy.
3 You get assigned to put down a revolt in one of the Empire's provinces, roll either Persuade or Leadership if you fail decrease Soc by 1 and gain a Enemy.
4 Your commanding officer does not see you favourably due to his report you get a -4 DM to your next Advancement roll.
5 You are blamed by a senior mage for a complex ritual failing, as a punishment the next time you would be awarded a new spell, you will be denied.
6 You are injured lightly in combat, you need to recover but there are no obvious side effects.
